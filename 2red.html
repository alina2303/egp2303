2. Bestemte artikel

  • Blå
  • Rød
  • Grøn

Engelsk har bestemt artikel:

ved en præposition + et verdenshjørne,

The sun rises in the east and sets in the west.
The wind is blowing from the north.

ved en præposition+ left eller right,

You must turn to the right, not to the left.

ved grammatiske betegnelser,

The verb is either in the present or the past.
This noun is in the singular, not the plural.
Put this noun in the genitive.

ved navne på hoteller, restauranter, teatre, biografer, skibe, aviser.

The Ritz is a very famous hotel.
They are showing the film at the Odeon.
The Titanic sank although it was brand new.
The Times is probably England's most famous newspaper.

Engelsk har ikke bestemt artikel:

ved man og woman brugt som begreb,

Man is the most intelligent of all creatures (’mennesket’).
God created man and woman (manden og kvinden som 'arter').

ved måltider,

Breakfast is the most important meal of the day.
Dinner is served from 5 pm to 8 pm.

NB! Når der menes et bestemt måltid, skal artiklen med.

The dinner we had at that restaurant was awful!

ved årstider, højtider, ugedage, og som regel night og day.

Spring is the nicest of all the seasons.
He washes his hair on Fridays.
We always spend Easter and Christmas in London.
He has been out all night.

NB! Ved en bestemt årstid, højtid osv. skal artiklen med.

Do you remember the winter of 1994?
I was there the night my uncle died.

Substantiver, som betegner transportmidler og institutioner, har ikke artikel, når man tænker på deres funktion eller den handling, der foregår der. Tænker man derimod på en bestemt ting/bygning eller et bestemt sted, skal artiklen med. Dette gælder også for ord med lignende ’dobbeltfunktion’ (f.eks. table, bed, class, sea, home).

Ann is ill in bed ↔ The bed is in the next room
(funktionen ’at ligge i sengen’ ↔ selve møblet)
She went to church every Sunday ↔ She walked into the church at 3 pm.
(til gudstjeneste ↔ bygningen)
He's still in school ↔ The school is old
(modtager undervisning/går i skole ↔ bygningen)
He went by bus ↔ The bus was late this morning)
(transportmåde ↔ en bestemt bus)
