16. Henførende stedord

  • Blå
  • Rød
  • Grøn

Definerende eller parentetisk?

Engelsk har to typer henførende sætninger, som vi ikke altid skelner mellem på dansk: definerende og parentetiske. Der er flere væsentlige forskelle mellem de to typer sætninger.

Definerende henførende sætning:

En definerende henførende sætning præciserer, hvem/hvad der tales om.

The man that gave me the apple was very old.
The man who gave me the apple was very old.

Fjerner man den henførende sætning, er det ikke længere klart, hvem/hvad der tales om.

The man was very old. - What man? The man that gave me the apple!

Man kan bruge who/that/whose om personer og which/that/whose om ting.

Modsat dansk er der aldrig kommaer foran eller rundt om en definerende sætning (uanset om den indledes med who, which, that eller whose).

Where is the boy who was told to get me an aspirin?
The money was in the bag which was stolen.
The fish that Peter caught was smaller than mine.
I want the kitten whose nose is pink.

På dansk bruger vi tit et påpegende stedord foran en definerende sætning. Engelsk bruger det bestemte kendeord. Det henførende stedord kan ofte udelades.

the man (that) I love
den mand, (som) jeg elsker

Parentetisk henførende sætning:

En parentetisk henførende sætning definerer ikke det ord, den hører til. Den er blot en yderligere oplysning.

My cat, which is now very old, has just had five kittens.
My father, who is 45, thinks that young people are terrible.
Paul Simmons, who is in town this week, will give a talk on the difference between African and Indian elephants.

Man kan i alle tilfældene udelade den henførende sætning, uden at det forstyrrer forståelsen: jeg har kun én kat/én far osv., så de skal ikke defineres.

Man kan aldrig bruge that i en parentetisk sætning.

Why don’t you come over and see my new baby brother, who is only 5 days old.
When I entered the house, I was met by my mother-in-law, who had come down for the weekend.
Have you seen my new sweater, which has cost me a fortune?

Der er altid kommaer foran/rundt om en parentetisk sætning.

Peter Thistlethwaite, who is president of The Smokers’ Union, will give a talk at the church tomorrow.
I gave the book to my English teacher, who says that reading is very important.
Ronald Reagan, who was president of the United States, was an old man when he retired.
I alle sætningerne indgår en henførende sætning.
