3. Genitiv

  • Blå
  • Rød
  • Grøn

Engelsk har of-genitiv:

ved en person efterfulgt af en relativ sætning,

He is the son son of the woman who was here yesterday.
He is a student of the teacher who won a new car.
They were friends of the boy whose picture was in the newspaper.

når et ord, som kræver of-genitiv, står alene på dansk, skal man på engelsk omskrive med that of/those of.

The colour of the coat is the same as that of the trousers. (Frakkens farve er den samme som buksernes.)
The legs of the chair are taller than those of the sofa. (Stolens ben er højere end sofaens.)

Dobbelt genitiv:

Nu og da kan man på engelsk møde sætninger, som indeholder både s-genitiv og of-genitiv.

He is a mate of my brother's.
That song was a favourite of my father's.
She was wearing a hat of Susan's.

Engelsk kan have dobbelt genitiv, når man ønsker at fremhæve, at det, der ’ejes’ er én blandt flere.

my brother's mate → a mate of my brother's (én blandt flere kammerater)
my father's favorite → a favorite of my father's (én blandt flere favoritter)
Susan's hat → a hat of Susan's (én blandt flere hatte)

NB! En sætning med dobbelt genitiv kan udmærket siges med almindeligt s-genitiv, men så må man typisk omskrive med one of samt sætte det, der ejes, i pluralis.

He is one of my brother's mates.
That song was one of my father's favorites.
She was wearing one of Susan's hats.
