8. Futurum

  • Blå
  • Rød
  • Grøn

Nuanceret futurum:

Når man vil sige noget om futurum, er det mest almindelige at bruge will + grundformen af et verbum. Man kan dog danne futurum på andre måder, hvis man ønsker at udtrykke sig mere nuanceret om det, der vil ske:

be going to + grundformen af et verbum,
bruges til at udtrykke den nære futurum, det uundgåelige eller den talendes hensigt.

I am going to see her in ten minutes.
The sky is black. I think it is going to rain.
I am going to finish my book before I go to bed.

be about to + grundformen af et verbum,
bruges til at udtrykke den meget nære futurum (det man lige skal/skulle til at gøre).

I was about to open the door when I saw it was raining.
He was about to leave the country when the police caught him.
I was about to call the electricians when the lights suddenly came on again.

be to + grundformen af et verbum,
bruges til at udtrykke en plan, en aftale eller noget som skæbnen har bestemt.

He is to arrive on the 5:20 train.
I am to see the manager today.
We are all to die.
They are to hand in their papers tomorrow.

be + ing -form.
bruges stort set kun ved bevægelsesverber (to leave, to go, to come etc.) om noget, der er arrangeret.

I am leaving on the six-o'clock train.
When are you going to Spain?
He is coming to see me tomorrow.
Afgør hvilken fremtidsform som skal anvendes, når man oversætter de understregede verbaludtryk til engelsk.
