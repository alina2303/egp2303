17. Tegnsætning

  • Blå
  • Rød
  • Grøn

Engelsk har komma:

ved parentetiske/beskrivende henførende sætninger, men IKKE ved definerende,

My brother, who has just turned forty, is married to our cousin.
(beskrivende: Jeg har kun én bror, og han er i øvrigt lige blevet fyrre.)
My brother who has just turned forty is married to our cousin.
(definerende: Jeg har flere brødre, men det er ham, som lige er blevet fyrre.)

Students who are lazy ought to be beaten.
(definerende: De elever, som .... - og kun dem.)
Students, who are lazy, ought to be beaten.
(beskrivende: Elever, og de er i øvrigt allesammen dovne, skulle ha’ bank - altså alle elever, ikke kun de dovne.)

The bus (which) I take every morning is always full.
(definerende: Den bus, (som) jeg tager....)
I picked him up in my car, which is very old.
(beskrivende: Jeg har kun én bil, og den er i øvrigt gammel.)

NB! Sæt aldrig komma foran et henførende that. Det bruges jo netop kun i definerende sætninger.

The man that/who I want to marry has to be very rich.
One of the things that I brought home from Barcelona was a beautiful painting.

når which fører tilbage til en hel sætning,

ved ordene too og however,

I wanted to go, too but there wasn't room for me in the car.
I, too, wanted to go, but there wasn't room for me in the car.
However, Peter was invited to the party.
Peter, however, was invited to the party.
Peter was invited to the party, however.

efter en bisætning, som står foran en hovedsætning,

NB! Man sætter IKKE komma, hvis en bisætning kommer efter hovedsætningen. - Dette gælder dog ikke henførende sætninger.

efter et biord/biled, der står først i sætningen.
Jo længere ordet/biledet er, jo vigtigere er kommaet.

Two years ago, I was on a wonderful holiday in Greece.
In the town where I live, there is no university.
On the day of the funeral, the widow broke down completely.
Happily, all her children were there to comfort her.
On the west coast, just south of San Francisco, there are many sea lions.
On a rainy day, where you're all alone in the house, it's nice to stay in bed with a good book.
